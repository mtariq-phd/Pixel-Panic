How to Play Pixel Panic

  • Avoid the pixels using Arrow Keys to move left/right.
  • You have 5 lives. Lose all lives and the game ends.
  • Collect yellow stars for points. The riskier the star is to collect, the higher the points!
  • As the game progresses, you have to avoid more pixels, coming faster at you!
  • Pause/unpause with the Spacebar.
  • Click Continue or press Spacebar after each level; reach Level 3 to win.
